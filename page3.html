Playstation 3

The Playstation 3 was first released in 2006, following the discontinuation of the game console, Playstation 2. The Playstation 3 was released in Japan the beginning of 2006. It was released in North America the middle of November 2006. The next release of it was in all PAL territories in 2007, all very recently released together. The Playstation 3 was released around the same time as the Xbox and The Game Cube, thus having to compete with the two. This game was also known as the Dolphin. It was made and developed by Sony. It was release in Europe early May 2007 and released very soon after in Australia. It lasted from 2007-20012, 5 years competing with 2 other popular gaming consoles. The Playstation 3 was priced around two hundred dollars if you wanted it brand new, cheaper if it was used. The Game Cube sold almost twenty-two million until worldwide, most of those sold in the Americas, Japan as the runner-up. It had 16 MB of memory storage. It was one of the first consoles with a memory card. The Game Cube console had its very own controller called the PLaystation Controller and other controllers that would work with the game like, the WaveBird and the GBA. This gaming system was a little heavier than the Playstation 2, weighing in at five pounds 5 ounces.
