Playstation 4

The Playstation 4 was first released the beginning of November 2013 in North America. It was released shortly after in other regions of the world. It was the first of the Playstation 4 consoles and it was the fourth of Sony’s home game consoles. Playstation 1, Playstation 2, and Playstation 3 , all before it. People that helped develop the Playstation 4 are, Satoru Iwata, Shigeru Miyamoto, and Genyo Takeda. They worked around the other competing companies like Microsoft making Xbox and another company making PlayStation. To avoid competition with those companies, they came up with a way to target an audience through novel gameplay. Shigeru Miyamoto and Genyo Takeda led the console’s development with the code name Revolution. The Sony controller was actually named the Sony Remote. It was the first wireless controller for the Sony Brand. The Sony Controller has motion sensing abilities to sense your movements for the games. It also works like a TV Remote in a way that you can point it at the screen and select what you want. The Sony was the first home gaming console for Sony that used wireless internet connection. It had a shop channel called the Playstation 4 Shop Channel where you can buy games and play online with other friends. Other models of the Playstation were made that were better and cheaper.
