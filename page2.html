Playstation 2

The Playstation 2/i> was the successor to the Playstation 1. It came out in 2000, in the U.S. It came out in 2000 in Japan and 2001 in Europe and Australia. It lasted from 2000-2006, being discontinued in April of 2006. 32.93 million units were sold worldwide, most of them sold in the Americas. It had a 16-bit sound system and holds 4-64 MB of storage. It had a specific controller known as the Ps 2 Controller. It was very light, weighing at only 2.42 pounds. The best-selling game that was accompanied with the Playstation 2 was Crash Bandicoot, which sold over 11 million copies. The development of this gaming console started in 1997, 3 years before it was put out to the public. It was partnered with Silicon Graphics. The development was finished in 1998 but was but off until 2000 to finish the accompanying games with it. Originally charcoal gray, other varieties of colors came out after the originals were produced. The original has under 4 MB and if wanted, there was an Expansion Pak could be bought to expand the MB. This would also improve the quality of the game, so it was a good idea to get it when you get the game! Time magazines named it the Machine of the year as soon as it came out. It remained this way until 2011 when another company announced it as the 9th greatest video game console of all time! This puts it in the top 10. It was discontinued in 2006 only because another game console came out, named Playstation 3.
